Diamandi is a bespoke grillz and jewellery service. All pieces are made to order and customised to the individual client.

2. Consultations & Deposits

A £30 consultation deposit is required to secure your appointment. This deposit is non-refundable if you cancel with less than 24 hours notice or fail to attend.

If you attend your consultation and choose to proceed with an order, the £30 deposit will be deducted from your final balance.

If you attend but decide not to proceed with an order, the £30 deposit is retained as a consultation fee.

3. Orders & Payment

All prices quoted are per tooth and are confirmed at consultation. A quote will be provided before any production begins.

Full payment is required to begin work. This is non-refundable once production has commenced.

Accepted payment methods: Cash, Card, Payment link, Bank transfer.

All pieces are handmade to order. Production timelines are confirmed at consultation and vary depending on the design, material, and current workload.

Diamandi will make every reasonable effort to meet agreed timelines. However, we cannot be held liable for delays caused by factors outside of our control.

Any changes to a confirmed order after production has begun may incur additional charges and extend the timeline.

5. Fit & Alterations

All grillz are made from a dental impression taken at your consultation. If ordering a mould kit, it is the client's responsibility to ensure the impression is taken accurately.

One complimentary minor adjustment is offered within 14 days of collection if the fit is not as expected, provided the piece has not been damaged or altered.

Any significant changes to fit resulting from changes to the client's teeth after the impression was taken will be chargeable.

6. Returns & Refunds

Due to the bespoke and custom nature of all pieces, we are unable to offer refunds or exchanges once production has commenced.

If a piece is found to have a manufacturing defect, please contact us within 7 days of receipt. Defective pieces will be repaired or remade at no charge.

Grillz are personal items worn in the mouth. For hygiene reasons, all sales are final once a piece has been worn.

Diamandi does not use plated metals unless explicitly agreed in writing with the client.

Grillz are removable jewellery accessories and are not intended as a permanent dental fitting. Diamandi is not responsible for any dental issues arising from use.
