Questions

FAQ

Do I need to visit in person? ▼
Yes — we take your dental impression in person to guarantee a perfect fit. Postal impression kits are available for clients outside the area; contact us to arrange.
How long does it take? ▼
Standard turnaround is 7–10 working days from the day we take your impression. Express 3–5 day service is available for an additional charge.
Are the diamonds real? ▼
Yes. We use genuine VVS-grade diamonds and supply a certificate of authenticity with every diamond set. We do not use moissanite or cubic zirconia unless specifically requested.
Do you do payment plans? ▼
Yes — we offer a 50% deposit to begin, with the remainder due on collection. For larger orders we can discuss a staged payment plan at consultation.
Is it safe to wear grillz? ▼
Our grillz are removable and non-permanent. They're cast from food-safe metals and fitted precisely so they don't damage teeth. We recommend removing them while eating and cleaning them regularly.
